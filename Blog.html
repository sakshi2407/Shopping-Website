Guide to Shopping Spring Trends in 2022

The sun is out, hemlines are rising, and bright, bold colors are dominating the streets. It can mean only one thing: Spring 2022 fashion trends are finally here. Time to pack up gray knits and tuck away those wool trousers—the clothing that will carry you through the spring 2022 season has an upbeat, offbeat quality to it. Seen at runway shows from New York to Tokyo, the trends of spring 2022 embrace vibrancy and joy. At Tory Burch, bold stripes in black and white—some cut with a flash of grassy green—signaled a new quirky order. Prada and Chanel put swimsuits and short-shorts on the catwalk, proving that beachwear can be ready-to-wear, while Marine Serre and Dries Van Noten revived the popcorn tops of the ’90s to bring some fun into our closets.

Beat The Heat With White Hot Luxe.

When the days are long and the heat is rising, a wardrobe of crisp, cool whites is the perfect refreshment. Whether you’re by the beach or pounding the city streets, an all-white outfit solves a few key problems: you don’t have to think too much about coordinating the various components of your look and even if you break a sweat, you’ll still look put together.An all-white outfit provides a clean aesthetic that can be casual as a coordinated lounge set or elevated with a mixture of textures. Even with a minimalist, monochromatic palette, you’ll find endlessly creative ways to wear the colour across occasions, price points, and style sensibilities.With summer in full swing, there’s no better time to invest in hot weather staples.
